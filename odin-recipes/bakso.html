Bakso tikus kuah rondap

Indulge in the yummy goodness of meatball soup with bouncy meat and flavorful broth that will satisfy your cravings. This tasty clear meatball soup recipe will make every bite memorable. Learn how to make delicious meatball broth at home and experience the special treat of homemade meatballs. This simple meatball soup recipe is packed with chewy meat and savory broth. Follow these easy steps to make delicious meatballs with common ingredients at home.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams ground beef
  • 100 grams ice cubes, finely crushed with the ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons fried shallots
  • 2 tablespoons fried garlic
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 150 grams sago flour
